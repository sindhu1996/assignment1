From starting a small mathematics coaching center in 1979 to establishing a monolith of myriad and dynamic academic institutions, Dr. Ponguru Narayana has come a long way in pioneering what is today the Narayana Group of Educational Institutions, known best for its exceptional quality and holistic development.

Hailing from the coastal town of Nellore in Andhra Pradesh, P. Narayana is a post-graduate gold medalist in Statistics from S.V University, Tirupathi, who began his career with a humble vision to train young minds towards discernable achievements in science and technology.

As favourable results showed persistently, the scope of his vision expanded in multitudinous folds, contributing ever since to the growth of his academic ventures. From residential schools to professional colleges and coaching center across the country today, Dr. P. Narayana’s institutional envoy is transforming conventional and categorized education into an interdisciplinary confluence for the best scholastic and non-scholastic personality development.

Under his guidance, the Narayana Group has gathered reputation for being one of the most preferred educational destinations for securing top ranks in various competitive examinations at state, national and international levels.

Driven by the ambition to create young and dynamic individuals ever year, the institution founded by Dr. Narayana is etching strong impressions in the annals of India’s progress in education. Dr. P. Narayana is an epitome in the country for good leadership qualities, intellectual prowess, scientific temper and effective oratory. He is also known for his charitable disposition in financially supporting meritorious and economically backward students for continual of their education. The most illustrative representation of his social-good and administrative capabilities is the fact that he is the serving cabinet minister of Administration, Urban Planning and Development for the newly formed state of Andhra Pradesh. Despite taking up varied responsibilities, Dr. Narayana’s missionary zeal to work tirelessly for academia in common cause remains undeterred against all odds.
